<h3>NOVENA PRAYERS TO SAINT RITA OF CASCIA</h3>
    <p>
        <b>Leader:</b> Come, Holy Spirit, fill the hearts of your faithful, and enkindle in them the fire of 
            Your love. Send forth Your Spirit and our hearts will be renewed
    </p>
    <p>
       <b>All:</b> And you will renew the face of the earth.
    </p>

    <p><b>Leader:</b> O God, who by the light of the Holy Spirit, instructed the hearts of the faithful; 
        grant us, by the same Holy Spirit, a love and desire for what is right and just, and the constant 
        enjoyment of His consolation, we ask this through Christ our Lord. Amen.</p>

        <li> Holy Mary, Mother of pure love, pray for us.</li>
        <li> Holy Mary, Comforter of the afflicted, pray for us..</li>
        <li>Holy Mary, Queen of all saints, pray for us.</li>
        <li>Holy Mary, Model of life for Saint Rita, pray for us.</li>
        <li>St. Rita, our advocate and protectress, pray for us.</li>
        <li>St. Rita, beloved by the Lord, pray for us.</li>
        <li>St. Rita, given special grace from heaven, pray for us.</li>
        <li>St. Rita, remarkable in childhood, pray for us.</li>
        <li>St. Rita, model of obedience to God’s Will, pray for us.</li>
        <li>St. Rita, the peacemaker, pray for us.</li>
        <li>St. Rita, of untiring patience, pray for us.</li>
           <li> St. Rita, model of Christian mothers, pray for us.</li>
           <li> St. Rita, mirror for Christian spouses, pray for us.</li>
           <li> St. Rita, heroic in sacrifice, pray for us.</li>
           <li> St. Rita, generous in forgiving, pray for us.</li>
           <li> St. Rita, martyr in penitence, pray for us.</li>
           <li> St. Rita, embracing humility and poverty, pray for us.</li>
           <li> St. Rita, exemplary as a widow, pray for us.</li>
           <li> St. Rita, prompt to answer the divine call, pray for us.</li>
           <li> St. Rita, patient in suffering, pray for us.</li>
           <li> St. Rita, mirror of religious observance, pray for us.</li>
           <li> St. Rita, mystical rose of every virtue, pray for us.</li>

           <li> St. Rita, pierced with a thorn, pray for us.</li>

           <li> St. Rita, consumed with Divine Love, pray for us.</li>

           <li> St. Rita, incorrupt in your chaste body, pray for us.</li>

           <li> St. Rita, persevering in prayer, pray for us.</li>

           <li> Lamb of God, Who takes away the sins of the world, Have mercy on us</li>
    </ul>  

    <p><b>Leader: </b> Lord, you have signed your servant, Rita</p>
    <p><b>All:</b> With the marks of Your love and Passion</p>

    <p><b>Leader:</b> Let us pray. O God, who bestowed on St. Rita such grace that she loved her enemies, 
        and bore in her heart and on her forehead the mark of Your love and Passion, grant us, we 
        beseech You, through her merits and intercession, a love for our enemies. Through our 
        contemplation of Your Son’s sufferings and Passion, may we merit the reward promised to 
        the meek and the suffering. We ask this through Christ our Lord. Amen</p>
        
    <b>
        Prayer of Petition
    </b>
    <br>
    <p>All: Glorious St. Rita, patroness of those in need, your intercession with our Lord is most powerful. 
        Through the favors obtained by your prayers you have been called “Advocate of hopeless and even 
        impossible cases.” St. Rita, humble and pure; patient and compassionate Lover of Christ Crucified! 
        We have confidence that everyone who has recourse to you, will find comfort and relief. Listen to 
        our petitions and show your power with God in our behalf. Obtain our petitions for us if they are 
        for the greater honor of God, and for our good. We promise, if our petitions are granted, to make 
        known your favor, and to glorify God for His gift.
        Relying on your power with the merciful Savior, we ask of you…
        (Offer your requests in silence)
        By the singular merits of your childhood, obtain our request for us.
        By your perfect union with the Divine Will, obtain our request for us.
        By your acceptance of troubles in your married life, obtain our request for us.
        By the anguish felt at the murder of your husband, obtain our request for us.
        By the example of forgiveness you gave to your sons, obtain our request for us.
        By your miraculous entrance into the convent, obtain our request for us.
        By your daily penance and fasting, obtain our request for us.
        By your courage and joy in bearing the mark of the Crucified Savior,
        obtain our request for us.
        By the Divine Love which consumed your life, obtain our request for us.
        By your devotion in receiving the Blessed Sacrament, obtain our request for us.
        By the happiness you felt in leaving this life for union with Christ, obtain our
        request for us.
        By the example you have given to people of every state of life, obtain our request
        for us.</p>

       <p> <b>Leader: </b> Pray for us, St. Rita,</p>
        <p><b>All:</b> That we may be worthy of the promises of Christ.</p>
        <p><b>Leader: </b> Let us pray. </p>
        <p> <b>All: </b> Heavenly Father! In Your infinite love and mercy you heed the prayer of Your beloved servant, 
        Rita. You graciously grant favors through her intercession, which are considered impossible to 
        human skill and effort. Relying on her compassionate love, we ask You to assist us in our trials and 
        difficulties. Let unbelievers know that you are the helper of the humble, the defender of the weak, 
        and the strength of those who trust in You. We ask this through Christ our Lord. Amen
        </p>
